I've always seeked out the 'why' behind a 'what', more so since my professional experience in investment banking. Beyond the daily grind of settling trades and mitigating liquidity risks, it was interesting to identify patterns unfold. Patterns like 3x more settlement failures post central bank rerates, or increase in trade volume after a prominent election result, or unusually quiet days post Brexit. The curiosity to understand and quantify these phenomena with data, has brought me into the Analytics and Data Science space.
